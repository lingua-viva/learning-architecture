# Still I Rise — Organization Context

**Status**: `observed` — researched, not yet engaged

---

## Overview

Independent humanitarian organization providing world-class education to refugee and vulnerable children since 2018. Nominated for the Nobel Peace Prize (2023). 76,626 people reached globally.

**Founded by**: Nicolò Govoni (CEO)
**Funding model**: 100% private donations. Refuses government, supranational, and unethical corporate funding. 95%+ of donations go directly to programs (< 5% overhead).

---

## Schools

### International Schools (IB Pathway — Free)
First in the world to offer International Baccalaureate to refugee/vulnerable children free of charge.

| School | Location | Status | Focus |
|--------|----------|--------|-------|
| Nairobi | Kenya | Open (2021), IB World School (2024) | Refugee & vulnerable children — most established campus |
| Bogotá | Colombia | Open (2024) | Venezuelan and Colombian displaced children |
| Mumbai | India | Opening 2026 | Girls' education |
| Italy | Italy | Opening 2026 | Migrant, Roma, and vulnerable Italian children |

### Earlier Programs
Greece (Samos), Syria, Congo, Yemen — emergency education and community programs.

## Educational Philosophy

- **Mission**: "Among the forgotten children of today, we raise the leaders of tomorrow"
- **Curriculum**: IB (International Baccalaureate) — inquiry-based, internationally recognized
- **Values**: Ambition, Bravery, Care (ABC)
- **Method**: "Revolutionary Educational Method" — proprietary, details not public
- **Approach**: World-class education, not charity education. The same quality as any elite international school, but free.

---

## What Makes This Context Unique

1. **Students with interrupted education** — many have never attended formal school, or have years-long gaps
2. **Multilingual classrooms** — students may speak Somali, Swahili, Arabic, French, Tigrinya, Oromo, Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi — with no common L1
3. **Trauma history** — war, displacement, family separation, poverty
4. **Fiercely independent** — they reject dependency on any external entity. Any tool we provide must be owned by them.
5. **Multiple continents** — four campuses across Africa, South America, Asia, and Europe with different infrastructure, languages, and cultural contexts
6. **Opening an Italy campus in 2026** — migrant, Roma, and vulnerable Italian children. This is where our Italian education expertise (Lingua Viva, La Scuola framework) is directly relevant.
