# Engagement Approach

**Status**: `designed` — proposed, not yet agreed

---

## Three Phases

### Phase 1: Assessment (2-3 weeks)
**Goal**: Understand their context deeply enough to propose specific AI interventions.

- Map their current curriculum structure across campuses
- Interview teachers: what's their biggest time drain? What breaks?
- Identify where AI can reduce burden (not where it's theoretically interesting)
- Assess infrastructure: internet, devices, existing tools (Toddle? Google Workspace? Paper?)
- Deliver: gap analysis + specific recommendations

**Deliverable**: Assessment report with 3-5 specific AI interventions ranked by impact and feasibility.

### Phase 2: Pilot (2-3 months)
**Goal**: Deploy and validate with a small group before scaling.

- Build client-specific ontology for their curriculum
- Deploy Mission Canvas for 2-3 teachers at one campus (Nairobi — most established)
- Teacher workflow: query MC for curriculum guidance, lesson adaptation, assessment support
- Train teachers on the system — they must be self-sufficient
- Measure: teacher time saved, curriculum coverage, teacher satisfaction

**Deliverable**: Pilot report with measured outcomes + go/no-go recommendation for scale.

### Phase 3: Scale (6-12 months)
**Goal**: Roll out across campuses, add capabilities.

- Deploy to all campuses
- Add multilingual knowledge base
- Build student progress tracking (CEFR-based)
- Integrate with existing tools
- Train local administrators to maintain the system
- Transition ownership: they run it, we support

**Deliverable**: Running system owned and operated by Still I Rise.

## Pricing Context

Still I Rise is donation-funded (95%+ goes to programs). Pricing must reflect:
- They are not a commercial customer
- The work has social impact value beyond revenue
- A pilot must prove value before asking for scale investment
- Grant funding may be appropriate for Phase 2-3

Phase 1: $10-25K (assessment)
Phase 2: $50-100K (pilot)
Phase 3: Pricing TBD based on pilot outcomes
